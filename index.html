About Jeff

The Mentalist Jeff Blain has been capturing the imagination of his audience for more than 30 years.

Although less known by the general public since he does not perform on public stage or on TV, he is well known and widely respected in the field by other magicians and mentalists, not only here in Canada, but worldwide by the people in the business. Jeff is one of the pioneers of mentalism in Quebec. He was also a consultant for television and film.

He masters close-up mentalism (manipulation, influence, mind reading) and stand-up magic using sleight of hands. His vast magical background combined with his dynamic display of showmanship and his certification in NLP (Neuro Linguistic Programming) enables Jeff to influence human perceptions using techniques in psychology and hypnosis to make you believe anything is possible!

Jeff performs at corporate functions, private parties and special events for major corporations across Canada, USA, France and Mexico. His charisma, his attention and his ability to captivate people and to make them interact with each other is a guaranteed success and the highlight of every evening.

Conferences, promotional events, cocktails, seminars and conventions are miraculously transformed into memorable and inspirational events.

A lot more than magic!

The Services

The Cocktail

Perfect for gatherings of 40+ guests, Jeff will circulate through your event, engaging groups with astonishing mind reading and magical interactions. His performances naturally encourage guests to interact, creating an atmosphere of wonder and connection that transforms ordinary events into unforgettable experiences.

The Trade Show

Looking to promote your company's product or impress your clients? Jeff will integrate your product directly into his performances, creating a memorable branded experience. Your guests will receive promotional items as lasting reminders of your distinguished event and unforgettable company.

The Fundraiser

Take your fundraiser to the next level with Jeff's unique interactive formula. Guests play against the Mentalist, placing bets that he'll double if he loses. This engaging approach accumulates funds quickly while keeping everyone thoroughly entertained throughout your event.
